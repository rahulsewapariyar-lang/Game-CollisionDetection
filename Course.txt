Why is GLAD included before GLFW?

This is one of the most common interview questions.

GLFW/glfw3.h may include the old Windows OpenGL headers unless told otherwise. We want GLAD to provide the OpenGL declarations instead.

So the rule is:

#include <glad/glad.h>
#include <GLFW/glfw3.h>

Always.
